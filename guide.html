PHASER

Game guide for surviving longer, upgrading smarter, and staying alive against increasingly dangerous waves of enemies.

▶ Play Phaser ← Back to Anicable Studios

Controls

Desktop

Move: WASD

Fire: Left Click

Exit Station: E

Pause / Settings: ESC

Mobile

Move: Tap and drag on the left side of the screen.

Fire / Aim: Tap and drag on the right side of the screen.

Exit Station: Exit button in the bottom right.

Pause / Settings: Pause button in the top right.

Enemies

Arrow

Basic enemy that aggressively follows and rams your ship.

Zoid

Keeps distance and attacks from afar with ranged shots.

Kite

Sniper enemy with one powerful shot that charges up before firing.

Power Ups

Shield

Blocks all incoming damage — except from black holes.

Objects & Obstacles

Station

Upgrade your ship and recover inside a temporary safe zone.

Rings

Fly through them to gain experience and receive a speed boost.

Black Holes

Extremely dangerous — avoid them or use them to slingshot across space.

Tips

✔ Keep moving — staying still makes you an easy target.

✔ Use rings early to level up faster.

✔ Visit stations often to upgrade before difficulty spikes.

✔ Use black holes strategically for speed boosts, but do not get too close.

✔ Prioritize avoiding damage over chasing enemies.
