PHASER GAME GUIDE

Survive as long as possible while upgrading your ship and fighting increasingly difficult enemies.

▶ Play Phaser

Desktop Controls

Move: WASD

Fire: Left Click

Exit Station: E

Pause / Settings: ESC

Mobile Controls

Move:Tap and drag on left side of screen.

Fire/Aim: Tap and drag on right side of screen

Exit Station: Exit button (bottom right)

Pause / Settings: Pause button (top right)

Enemies

Arrow

Basic enemy that aggressively follows and rams your ship.

Zoid

Keeps distance and attacks from afar with ranged shots.

Kite

Sniper enemy, one powerful shot that charges up before firing.

Power Ups

Shield

Blocks all incoming damage — except from black holes.

Objects & Obstacles

Station

Upgrade your ship and recover in a safe zone.

Rings

Fly through them to gain experience and a speed boost.

Black Holes

Extremely dangerous — avoid them or use them to slingshot across space.

Tips

✔ Keep moving — staying still makes you an easy target.

✔ Use rings early to level up faster.

✔ Visit stations often to upgrade before difficulty spikes.

✔ Use black holes strategically for speed boosts (high risk, high reward).

✔ Prioritize avoiding damage over chasing enemies.
